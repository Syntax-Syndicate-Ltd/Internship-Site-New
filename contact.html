Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

🆓 Is Syntax Syndicate free to use?

Yes, completely. Browsing and applying to opportunities will always be free for developers.

📋 How do I list my company's opportunity?

Send us an email or fill the contact form with "List an Opportunity" as the subject. Our team reviews and publishes it within 48 hours.

✅ How are listings verified?

Every listing is manually reviewed by our admin team before going live. We check that the apply link is active and the information is accurate.

📣 Can I share opportunities I find?

Absolutely! Found something great? Contact us and we'll consider adding it to the platform for the entire community.

🔒 Is my data safe?

Yes. We only store your email, name, and college for account purposes. We never sell your data. Read our Privacy Policy.

🐛 I found a bug, who do I tell?

Please use the contact form and select "Report an Issue". We appreciate every bug report — it helps us improve for everyone.
