Our Story

Built for Developers,
By Developers

Syntax Syndicate was born from a simple frustration — great opportunities were scattered everywhere, and finding them took more time than it should.

Our Mission

Making opportunities accessible to every developer

We believe that talent is evenly distributed, but opportunity is not. Students in tier-2 and tier-3 cities often miss out on great internships, hackathons, and jobs simply because they don't know they exist.

Syntax Syndicate curates, verifies, and surfaces the best tech opportunities — jobs, internships, hackathons, tech events, seminars, and courses — all in one sleek, distraction-free platform.
